Documents to Carry

When your railway ticket booking is confirmed with BharathYatra, you need to carry your government recognized ID on the departure date. Train ticket-checker may come any time and may ask you to show railway booking along with your ID. Here is the list of IDs that you can carry while boarding the train:

  • Aadhar Card
  • Voter ID
  • Pan Card
  • Passport
  • Driving License
  • Bank passbook with photograph
  • Student ID card with photograph
